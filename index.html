Tasman Manx

The Tasman Manx is a rare breed, known for their curly fur and short tails. They are believed to have originated from the Isle of Man. Records from the Isle of Man say that the Tasman Manx was the product of a mutation among the islands domestic cats. They then ended up in Tasman, New Zealand. The Tasman Manx is affectionate, mellow, and even tempered.
